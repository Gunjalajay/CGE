Fixed resistors

The fixed resistor type is the most common resistor, when people talk about a resistor they most probably mean a fixed resistor. The picture to the right shows an axial carbon film resistor, the most common type. Fixed resistors have a constant resistance value. Different resistor materials are used for fixed resistors. For all resistor types the used materials has influence on the resistor properties like the tolerance, cost and noise. Fixed resistors are available in axial and SMD packages.
